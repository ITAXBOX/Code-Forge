Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Code Forge?

Simply click the "Get Started" button on our homepage and follow the step-by-step process to generate your first project. No credit card required for the free tier.

What technologies does Code Forge support?

We support multiple frontend frameworks (React, Vue, Angular), backend technologies (Spring Boot, Node.js), and databases (MySQL, MongoDB).

How long does project generation take?

Most projects generate in under 2 minutes, depending on complexity. Very large projects with many entities may take up to 5 minutes.

Can I customize the generated code?

Absolutely! The generated code is completely yours to modify. We provide clean, well-structured code that's easy to extend and customize for your specific needs.

What if I need help with my generated project?

Our support team can help with issues related to the generation process. For help customizing your project, we offer premium support plans with direct developer assistance.
